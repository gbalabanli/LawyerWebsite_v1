Legal Risk Checklist for Small Businesses

February 19, 2026 Attorney Aydin
business-lawcompliancerisk-management

Contents

  • Contract inventory
  • Employment documentation
  • Decision trail

Small businesses can reduce legal surprises with a recurring legal hygiene routine. A short monthly checklist is usually enough.

Contract inventory

Track renewal dates, automatic extension clauses, and open obligations for vendors and customers.

Employment documentation

Maintain updated role descriptions, policy acknowledgments, and performance records in a consistent format.

Decision trail

Important internal decisions should include rationale and approval notes to prevent later uncertainty.
