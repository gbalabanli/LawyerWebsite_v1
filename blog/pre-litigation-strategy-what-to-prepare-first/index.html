Pre-Litigation Strategy: What to Prepare First

March 28, 2026 Attorney Aydin
litigationevidencecivil-law

Contents

  • Preserve all records
  • Define your legal objective
  • Evaluate negotiation window

Many cases become stronger or weaker before filing. Collecting evidence early reduces risk and improves negotiation leverage.

Preserve all records

Keep signed documents, invoices, messages, emails, and timeline notes in one place. Missing chronology is a common weakness.

Define your legal objective

Clarify whether your main goal is recovery, injunction, settlement, or formal clarification of rights.

Evaluate negotiation window

In some disputes, a structured notice letter and clear deadline can resolve the matter before court action.
